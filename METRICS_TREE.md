# METRICS_TREE.md

## North Star
- Organic Effective Clicks (Google Search clicks to high-intent pages)

## KPI Tree

### 1) Outcome (business/result)
- Organic Clicks (weekly, WoW)
- Organic Impressions (weekly, WoW)
- CTR (weekly, WoW)
- Avg Position (weekly, WoW)
- Conversion proxy (contact click / outbound CTA click)

### 2) Output (execution)
- High-value posts published / week
- Existing posts refreshed / week
- Internal links added / week
- Indexed new pages / week

### 3) Quality (content quality gate)
- Content Scorecard average (0-30)
- % posts with score >= 20
- % posts with verifiable sources
- % posts with actionable 5-minute fix section

## Guardrails
- No placeholder content (`- ` / TBD / template headings)
- No “worklog-style weekly posts” (must be industry insight + user value)
- If score < 20: rework before promotion/distribution
