Telegram can be more than a chat app. For many teams, it becomes a low-friction AI operation console.

## 3 Business Workflows That Convert

### 1) Lead Qualification Assistant
- Capture inbound requests
- Auto-tag intent (pricing / support / partnership)
- Route hot leads to human sales immediately

### 2) Sales Follow-up Automation
- Draft contextual follow-up messages
- Trigger reminders when lead is inactive for 24/48 hours
- Keep all replies in one operator channel

### 3) Internal Ops Copilot
- Daily KPI summary in Telegram
- Ticket triage suggestions
- Quick command-based report generation

## Conversion Design Principles

- Keep commands short and task-specific
- Require confirmation for high-risk actions
- Track response time, closure rate, and cost per task
